Small Business, Big Ambition

Hi, I’m Agilan Arunkumar. While most people my age are focusing on video games, I’m focusing on building a legacy. I started this garden and cleaning service because I believe in the value of hard work and providing top-tier results for my neighbors.

Why

My goal is simple: to build a business from the ground up that eventually turns into something massive. Every lawn I mow and every driveway I shovel is a step toward my dream of becoming a self-made entrepreneur. When you hire me, you aren’t just getting a clean yard—you’re supporting a local student’s journey to the top.

My Guarantee To You

I know that trust is earned, not given. That’s why I have a strict 'No Pay Until You're Happy' policy. I don’t ask for money online or upfront. I do the work, you inspect it, and only then do we settle the bill. It’s the old-school way of doing business—built on honesty and a handshake.
